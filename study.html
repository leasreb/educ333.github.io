Arguments

Enhances Social Interaction:

Numerous studies have demonstrated that playing video games can enhance social interaction by providing opportunities for cooperative gameplay and communication. Active video games (AVGs) have emerged as a promising avenue for enhancing social interaction among players. Maitland et al. (2018) conducted a study utilizing the Social Interaction Potential Assessment (SIPA) tool, which evaluates various aspects of social interaction within video games. Their research revealed that multiplayer options, online capacity, and enjoyment significantly contribute to the social interaction potential of AVGs. Moreover, features such as cooperative play, competitive play, and altruism within game mechanics were found to promote social interaction among players. The study concluded that, "Overall the SIPA tool exhibited good inter- and intrarater reliability" (Maitland et al.,2018). The results of the study suggests that the design elements of video games play a crucial role in fostering social connections and facilitating collaborative experiences among gamers. Additionally, AVGs provide platforms where individuals can engage in shared activities, communicate with peers, and develop interpersonal skills in a virtual environment, thereby enhancing their overall social interaction abilities.

Furthermore, the study by Perry et al. (2018) explored the differential associations between various types of gaming relationships and social outcomes. Their findings indicate that online multiplayer games offer opportunities for collaboration and interdependent social relationships among players. Perry et al.'s articles states that the results of the study "emphasise that social interactions in (and around) online multiplayer video games are effective for building social capital, and do so by ensuring game play is in harmony with other goals and values" (Perry et al., 2018). Through processes such as bonding and bridging social capital, players establish and maintain social networks within gaming communities. These results highlight the multifaceted nature of social interaction in video games and also the importance of understanding the diverse ways in which players engage with one another within virtual environments. Overall, research suggests that AVGs have the potential to enhance social interaction by providing avenues for communication, cooperation, and shared experiences among players, therefore enriching their social lives both online and offline.

Facilitates Meaningful Relationships:

In addition to enhancing social interaction, video games have been shown to facilitate the formation of meaningful relationships among players. Perry et al. (2018) found that online multiplayer games provide opportunities for players to collaborate and develop interdependent social relationships. Through processes such as bonding and bridging social capital, players establish connections and strengthen social networks within gaming communities. These relationships extend beyond the confines of the game, often leading to friendships and social interactions outside the virtual world. Moreover, the study demonstrated that harmonious passion, characterized by positive engagement with the game, mediates increases in social capital, contributing to the formation of strong and lasting relationships among gamers.

Furthermore, research by Jensen (2017) suggests that game play style and social context play crucial roles in shaping the quality of relationships formed through video games. According to Jensen (2017), "Cooperative game play styles have been associated with increased prosocial behavior and the development of meaningful connections among players" (Jensen, 2017). This highligts the significance of cooperative gameplay in fostering positive social interactions and relationship development within gaming communities. It shows how this type of game play can lead to actual meaningful connections.Overall, video games provide platforms for players to engage in collaborative activities, communicate with peers, and cultivate profound relationships, thereby contributing to their overall well-being and social development.

Provides Platforms for Social Connections:

One significant aspect of video games is their ability to provide platforms for social connections, allowing individuals to interact and engage with others in meaningful ways. Maitland et al. (2018) identified various features within active video games (AVGs) that contribute to their potential for facilitating social connections. These features include multiplayer options, online capacity, and game enjoyment, all of which enable players to engage with a diverse community of gamers. By offering opportunities for cooperative and competitive play, video games create environments where individuals can connect with others, collaborate on tasks, and build relationships based on shared interests and experiences.

Video games offer a unique space where individuals can engage in social interactions regardless of geographical boundaries or physical limitations. By providing accessible and inclusive platforms for communication and collaboration, video games enable individuals from diverse backgrounds to come together, share experiences, and form connections based on mutual interests and experiences. Overall, video games serve as valuable tools for facilitating social connections, fostering a sense of community, and enriching the social lives of players worldwide. This can only be possible because the game itself is providing users with the environment to interact with others and build real connections.

Conlcusion:

In conclusion, the evidence presented displays the role of video games in enhancing social interaction, facilitating meaningful relationships, and providing platforms for social connections. The research by Maitland et al. (2018) highlights the capacity of active video games to facilitate positive social interaction, emphasizing the importance of social facilitators and pro-social features in gaming environments. Additionally, Perry et al. (2018) shed light on the pivotal role of online multiplayer games as platforms for establishing social networks, emphasizing the positive impact of harmonious passion on relationship formation. Finally, Jensen (2017) suggests that game play style and social context play crucial roles in shaping the quality of relationships formed through video games, emphasizing the significance of cooperative play styles in fostering meaningful connections among players. Collectively, these findings demonstrate the potential of video games in fostering social interaction, nurturing relationships, and creating positive online communities.
